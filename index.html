About Me


Hello! I'm Ahmed Ezz Eldeen, an Electrical and Control Engineer with 14 years of professional experience. I began my career as an Electrical Engineer in the electrical contracting industry, working on a wide range of electrical installation and infrastructure projects. I currently work at South Delta Electricity Distribution Company (SDEDC). Throughout my career within the company, I have worked in several Electrical Engineering and Control departments as an Operations Engineer, gaining extensive hands-on experience in power distribution systems, field operations, and control processes. Later, I leveraged my engineering background and programming skills by moving into the Information Systems (IT) department, where I now work as a Software Engineer. My primary focus is developing C# desktop applications and Microsoft SQL Server database solutions that automate workflows, improve operational efficiency, and provide practical tools for operations engineers and other technical departments across the company. In addition to my engineering and software development experience, I have extensive expertise in PC hardware, including system building, troubleshooting, maintenance, and performance optimization. I am passionate about combining engineering knowledge with software development to create innovative, reliable, and efficient solutions that solve real-world engineering challenges.

My journey into software development began through my work as an Electrical and Control Engineer. I started learning programming to develop practical solutions for the challenges faced by operations engineers. Since then, I have continued to improve my programming skills and explore new technologies to build efficient and reliable engineering applications.
